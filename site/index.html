Milan Gross (SharePoint UG Lead, Singapore)

Milan Gross, SharePoint UG Lead, Singapore

Milan Gross is the head of the Asia Pacific arm of Synergy and a consultant and trainer with over 15 years experience as an architect and developer of Microsoft technologies. Milan is a frequent invited speaker at Tech Ed conferences throughout the Asia and Pacific region.

In addition to consulting, Milan is also a professional trainer delivering the Synergy technical SharePoint courses around the world. Helping others understand the technology is something he admits to being passionate about.

Milan is dedicated to building long-term customer relationships by leading a diverse team of highly-skilled professionals. As an experienced project manager, he works with his staff to keep all focused on constantly exceeding customer expectations.

Milan is based in Singapore where he assists in supporting the Singapore SharePoint User Group.
